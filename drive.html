ToolKit

Type I for Individual

Flow Test - A week, set 40 reminders in mobile, in each reminder write down what you are doing, what you feel and check whether the "flow". Before go to bed in every day, ask a question "Was I better today than yesterday?"

Give yourself a performance review

Do the following:

  1. Deliberate practice
  2. Repeat, repeat, repeat
  3. Seek constant, critical feedback
  4. Focus ruthlessely on where you need help
  5. Prepare for the process to be mentally and physically exhausting

Type I for Organization

Do the following:

  1. Involve people in goal setting
  2. Use non-controlling languages -> don't use "should" or "must", instead use "think about it" or "consider"
  3. Hold office hours

The Zen of compensation - Paying people the Type I way

Type I for parent and educators

Do the following:

  1. Have a FEDEX day
  2. Give your kid allowance and allow decide how to save it spend it
  3. Praise effort and strategy, not intelligence
  4. Make praise specific
  5. Praise in private
  6. Offer praise only when there's a good reason for it
